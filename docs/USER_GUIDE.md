## Gameplay Guide

### How to Play a Session

#### 1. Select a Category

- Browse the available categories on the home screen
- Each category shows:
  - Number of NFTs you've earned
  - Available NFT stock
  - Your perfect score count

#### 2. Start a Session

- Click "Start Session" on your chosen category
- You'll see the session rules:
  - **10 questions** per session
  - **10 seconds** per question
  - **No pauses** or skips allowed
  - **Perfect score = mint chance**

#### 3. Answer Questions

- Read each question carefully
- Select one of four options (A, B, C, or D)
- Watch the countdown timer (turns red at 3 seconds!)
- If time runs out, the answer is marked incorrect

**Tips**:
- Answer quickly but accurately
- The timer continues even if you refresh the page
- You can't change your answer once submitted

#### 4. View Results

After completing all 10 questions, you'll see:
- Your final score (out of 10)
- Total time taken
- Win/loss status (6+ correct = win)
- Perfect score bonus (if applicable)

### Session Limits

**Guest Players**:
- 5 sessions per day
- Resets at midnight ET

**Wallet-Connected Players**:
- 10 sessions per day
- Resets at midnight ET

**Cooldown Period**:
- 60 seconds between sessions
- Allows the system to process your results

### Scoring System

**Points Per Question**:
- Correct answer: 1 point
- Incorrect answer: 0 points
- Timeout: 0 points

**Perfect Score Bonus**:
- 10/10 correct: +10 bonus points (20 total)
- Grants mint eligibility for that category

**Win Conditions**:
- 6-9 correct: Win (no mint eligibility)
- 10 correct: Win + Mint Eligibility
- 0-5 correct: Loss

### Question Rotation

- Questions are randomly selected from a large pool
- You won't see the same question twice in one day
- After midnight ET, the rotation resets
- Questions maintain consistent difficulty

### Reporting Questions

If you encounter an ambiguous or incorrect question:

1. Click the "Report" button during or after the session
2. Select a reason:
   - Ambiguous wording
   - Multiple correct answers
   - Incorrect answer
   - Other issue
3. Add optional comments
4. Submit the report

Our team reviews all flagged questions and makes corrections as needed.

**Best Practices**:
- Never share your seed phrase with anyone
- Use a hardware wallet for large amounts

1. Disconnect your current wallet
2. Connect the new wallet
3. If it's a new stake key, you'll create a new profile
4. Your old profile remains accessible with the original wallet

## NFT Minting

### Earning Mint Eligibility

**How to Qualify**:
- Achieve a perfect score (10/10 correct answers)
- Complete the session within the time limits
- Have NFTs available in that category

**Eligibility Window**:
- **Wallet-connected players**: 1 hour to mint
- **Guest players**: 25 minutes to connect wallet and mint
- After expiration, the eligibility is lost

**Eligibility Limits**:
- One active eligibility per category at a time
- Multiple eligibilities across different categories allowed
- Must mint or wait for expiration before earning another in the same category

### Minting Process

#### Step 1: Achieve Perfect Score

- Complete a session with 10/10 correct answers
- You'll see: "Flawless! You've unlocked a [Category] mint. Claim within 1 hour."

#### Step 2: Initiate Mint

- Click "Mint Now" button
- Review the NFT details:
  - Category name
  - NFT artwork preview
  - Estimated transaction fee (~2 ADA)
- Click "Confirm Mint"

#### Step 3: Sign Transaction

- Your wallet will open with a transaction request
- Review the transaction details:
  - Receiving address (your wallet)
  - Transaction fee
  - NFT metadata
- Approve the transaction in your wallet

#### Step 4: Wait for Confirmation

- The minting process takes 2-5 minutes
- Progress indicators show:
  - Uploading metadata to IPFS
  - Building transaction
  - Submitting to blockchain
  - Waiting for confirmation

#### Step 5: Receive Your NFT

- Once confirmed, the NFT appears in your wallet
- View it in:
  - Your TriviaNFT profile
  - Your Cardano wallet
  - Cardano blockchain explorers

### NFT Details

**Metadata**:
- Name: "[Category] Trivia NFT #XXX"
- Image: Unique artwork stored on IPFS
- Attributes:
  - Category
  - Rarity (Common, Rare, Epic, Legendary)
  - Mint date
  - Season

**Policy ID**: All TriviaNFT NFTs share the same policy ID for authenticity

**Rarity Tiers**:
- **Common**: Standard category NFTs (most frequent)
- **Ultimate**: Forged from 10 category NFTs
- **Master**: Forged from 10 different categories
- **Seasonal**: Forged during active seasons

### Viewing Your NFTs

**In TriviaNFT**:
- Go to your Profile
- Click "My NFTs" tab
- Filter by category or tier
- View full metadata and artwork

**In Your Wallet**:
- Open your Cardano wallet
- Navigate to NFT section
- Find TriviaNFT collection

**On Blockchain Explorers**:
- Visit cardanoscan.io or pool.pm
- Search for your wallet address
- View all your NFTs with transaction history

### Troubleshooting Mints

**Mint Failed**:
- Check your ADA balance (need ~2-5 ADA)
- Ensure wallet is still connected
- Try again or contact support

**Mint Pending Too Long**:
- Blockchain confirmations can take 5-10 minutes during high traffic
- Check transaction status on cardanoscan.io
- If stuck after 30 minutes, contact support

**NFT Not Showing in Wallet**:
- Wait 5-10 minutes for wallet to sync
- Refresh your wallet
- Check on blockchain explorer to confirm mint
- Some wallets take longer to display NFT metadata

## NFT Forging

Forging allows you to combine multiple NFTs to create higher-tier, rarer NFTs. There are three types of forging available.

### Category Ultimate Forging

**Requirements**:
- Own 10 NFTs from the same category
- All NFTs must be in your wallet

**Process**:
1. Go to "Forge" section in your profile
2. Select the category you want to forge
3. Review the 10 NFTs that will be consumed
4. Click "Forge Category Ultimate"
5. Confirm the warning: "Forging will consume your NFTs permanently. Proceed?"
6. Approve the burn transaction in your wallet
7. Wait for burn confirmation (2-5 minutes)
8. Approve the mint transaction for the Ultimate NFT
9. Wait for mint confirmation (2-5 minutes)
10. Receive your Category Ultimate NFT!

**Result**:
- 10 common NFTs are burned (permanently destroyed)
- 1 Category Ultimate NFT is minted to your wallet
- Ultimate NFTs are rarer and more valuable

**Example**:
- Forge 10 Science NFTs ‚Üí 1 Science Ultimate NFT

### Master Ultimate Forging

**Requirements**:
- Own at least 1 NFT from 10 different categories
- All NFTs must be in your wallet

**Process**:
1. Go to "Forge" section in your profile
2. Click "Forge Master Ultimate"
3. Review the 10 NFTs (one from each category) that will be consumed
4. Confirm the warning
5. Approve burn transaction
6. Wait for confirmation
7. Approve mint transaction for Master Ultimate
8. Receive your Master Ultimate NFT!

**Result**:
- 10 NFTs (one from each category) are burned
- 1 Master Ultimate NFT is minted
- Master Ultimates are the rarest tier

**Example**:
- Forge 1 Science + 1 History + 1 Geography + ... (10 categories) ‚Üí 1 Master Ultimate NFT

### Seasonal Ultimate Forging

**Requirements**:
- Own 2 NFTs from each active category in the current season
- Must forge during the active season or within 7-day grace period
- All NFTs must be in your wallet

**Process**:
1. Go to "Forge" section in your profile
2. Click "Forge Seasonal Ultimate"
3. Review the 18 NFTs (2 from each of 9 categories) that will be consumed
4. Confirm the warning
5. Approve burn transaction
6. Wait for confirmation
7. Approve mint transaction for Seasonal Ultimate
8. Receive your Seasonal Ultimate NFT!

**Result**:
- 18 NFTs (2 per category) are burned
- 1 Seasonal Ultimate NFT is minted
- Seasonal Ultimates are exclusive to that season

**Important**:
- Seasonal forging is only available during the active season + 7 days
- After the grace period ends, you cannot forge seasonal NFTs
- Each season has unique Seasonal Ultimate artwork

### Forging Progress

Track your forging progress in your profile:

**Category Progress**:
- Shows NFTs collected per category (e.g., "7/10 Science")
- Progress bar indicates how close you are
- "Forge Ready" indicator when you have enough

**Master Progress**:
- Shows categories completed (e.g., "8/10 categories")
- Lists which categories you still need

**Seasonal Progress**:
- Shows NFTs per category for current season
- Displays time remaining in season
- Grace period countdown after season ends

### Forging Tips

**Strategy**:
- Focus on one category at a time for Category Ultimates
- Diversify across categories for Master Ultimate
- Plan seasonal forging early in the season

**Ownership Verification**:
- Forging checks on-chain ownership before proceeding
- If you transfer NFTs to another wallet, they won't count
- Receiving NFTs from others adds to your forging progress

**Transaction Fees**:
- Forging requires two transactions (burn + mint)
- Total fees: ~4-8 ADA
- Ensure you have enough ADA before starting

**Irreversible Process**:
- Once you confirm forging, NFTs are permanently burned
- There is no undo or refund
- Double-check your selection before confirming

## Leaderboards and Seasons

### Understanding Seasons

**What is a Season?**
- A 3-month competitive period
- Starts on the first day of each quarter (Jan 1, Apr 1, Jul 1, Oct 1)
- Named by season: Winter, Spring, Summer, Fall
- Each season has its own leaderboard and prizes

**Current Season Info**:
- View on the home screen or leaderboard page
- Shows season name, start/end dates, and countdown
- Displays active categories for seasonal forging

**Season Prizes**:
- Top player on the global leaderboard wins an exclusive NFT
- Prize is awarded at season end
- Only wallet-connected players with usernames are eligible

### Leaderboard Types

#### Global Leaderboard

**What It Shows**:
- All players ranked by total seasonal points
- Updated in real-time after each session
- Displays username, points, NFTs minted, perfect scores

**How to Rank Higher**:
- Earn more points (1 per correct answer)
- Achieve perfect scores (10-point bonus)
- Mint more NFTs
- Answer questions faster
- Use fewer sessions

**Tie-Breakers** (in order):
1. Total points
2. NFTs minted this season
3. Perfect scores this season
4. Average answer time (faster is better)
5. Fewer sessions used
6. Earlier achievement timestamp

#### Category Leaderboards

**What It Shows**:
- Players ranked by points in a specific category
- Separate leaderboard for each of the 9 categories
- Shows category-specific stats

**How to Access**:
- Click on a category
- Select "Leaderboard" tab
- View your rank in that category

#### Season History

**What It Shows**:
- Final standings from past seasons
- Season winners and their stats
- Your performance in previous seasons

**How to Access**:
- Go to Leaderboard page
- Select "Past Seasons" tab
- Choose a season to view

### Earning Points

**Points Per Session**:
- 1 point per correct answer
- 10-point bonus for perfect score (10/10)
- 0 points for incorrect answers
- Maximum 20 points per session

**Example Scoring**:
- 7/10 correct: 7 points
- 10/10 correct: 10 + 10 bonus = 20 points
- 5/10 correct: 5 points

**Points Accumulation**:
- Points accumulate throughout the season
- No points are subtracted for losses
- All sessions count toward your total

### Season Transitions

**What Happens at Season End**:
1. Current season leaderboard is finalized
2. Winner is announced and prize awarded
3. Leaderboard is archived for history
4. New season begins immediately
5. All players start at 0 points

**What Carries Over**:
- Your owned NFTs (all tiers)
- Active mint eligibilities (until they expire)
- Your username and profile
- All-time statistics

**What Resets**:
- Seasonal points (back to 0)
- Seasonal leaderboard rankings
- Seasonal forging eligibility (after grace period)

**Grace Period**:
- 7 days after season end
- Allows you to complete seasonal forging
- After grace period, seasonal forging is disabled

### Viewing Your Stats

**Profile Statistics**:
- Total sessions played
- Win/loss record
- Perfect score count (overall and per category)
- NFTs minted and forged
- Current season rank and points
- Historical season performance

**Activity Log**:
- Chronological list of all mints and forges
- Transaction hashes for verification
- Timestamps and NFT details

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is TriviaNFT free to play?**
A: Yes! You can play as a guest for free. To mint NFTs, you need a Cardano wallet with ADA for transaction fees (~2-5 ADA).

**Q: Do I need a Cardano wallet to play?**
A: No, you can play as a guest. However, you need a wallet to mint NFTs and appear on leaderboards.

**Q: What blockchain does TriviaNFT use?**
A: TriviaNFT uses the Cardano blockchain for all NFT minting and transactions.

**Q: How much ADA do I need?**
A: We recommend having 2-5 ADA in your wallet for transaction fees. Each mint costs approximately 2 ADA, and forging costs 4-8 ADA.

**Q: Can I play on mobile?**
A: Yes! TriviaNFT works on mobile browsers. For the best experience, use a wallet's dApp browser (Eternl, Typhon).

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**
A: Not yet, but we're working on native iOS and Android apps. The web version works great on mobile in the meantime.

### Gameplay Questions

**Q: How many questions are in each session?**
A: Every session has exactly 10 questions.

**Q: How much time do I have per question?**
A: You have 10 seconds to answer each question. The timer is visible and turns red at 3 seconds.

**Q: Can I pause a session?**
A: No, sessions cannot be paused. Once started, you must complete all 10 questions or forfeit.

**Q: What happens if I refresh the page during a session?**
A: Your session will resume where you left off, but the timer continues running. Try to avoid refreshing during active sessions.

**Q: How many sessions can I play per day?**
A: Guest players: 5 sessions per day. Wallet-connected players: 10 sessions per day. Limits reset at midnight ET.

**Q: What is the cooldown period?**
A: After completing a session, you must wait 60 seconds before starting another. This allows the system to process your results.

**Q: Will I see the same questions again?**
A: Not on the same day. Questions rotate daily, so you won't see repeats within 24 hours. After midnight ET, questions may reappear.

**Q: What score do I need to win?**
A: You need 6 or more correct answers (out of 10) to win a session. However, only a perfect score (10/10) grants mint eligibility.

**Q: Can I report incorrect questions?**
A: Yes! Click the "Report" button during or after a session. Our team reviews all flagged questions.

### NFT Questions

**Q: How do I earn an NFT?**
A: Achieve a perfect score (10/10 correct answers) in any category. This grants you mint eligibility for that category.

**Q: How long do I have to mint after a perfect score?**
A: Wallet-connected players have 1 hour. Guest players have 25 minutes to connect a wallet and mint.

**Q: What happens if I don't mint in time?**
A: The eligibility expires and the NFT returns to the available stock. You'll need to achieve another perfect score.

**Q: Can I have multiple mint eligibilities?**
A: Yes, across different categories. However, you can only have one active eligibility per category at a time.

**Q: Are the NFTs unique?**
A: Each NFT has unique metadata and artwork. While multiple NFTs exist per category, each has its own token ID and mint number.

**Q: Can I sell my NFTs?**
A: Yes! TriviaNFT NFTs are standard Cardano NFTs. You can trade them on any Cardano NFT marketplace (jpg.store, CNFT.io, etc.).

**Q: What is the policy ID?**
A: All TriviaNFT NFTs share the same policy ID for authenticity. You can find it in your NFT's metadata or on our website.

### Forging Questions

**Q: What is forging?**
A: Forging is the process of combining multiple NFTs to create a higher-tier, rarer NFT.

**Q: What happens to my NFTs when I forge?**
A: The input NFTs are permanently burned (destroyed) on the blockchain. You receive a new, higher-tier NFT in return.

**Q: Can I undo a forge?**
A: No, forging is irreversible. Once NFTs are burned, they cannot be recovered.

**Q: Do I need NFTs from the same season to forge?**
A: For Category and Master forging: No, NFTs from any season work. For Seasonal forging: Yes, NFTs must be from the current season.

**Q: Can I forge with NFTs I bought from someone else?**
A: Yes! Forging is based on current wallet ownership, not who originally minted the NFTs.

**Q: How much does forging cost?**
A: Forging requires two transactions (burn + mint), costing approximately 4-8 ADA total in transaction fees.

**Q: What if I transfer NFTs to another wallet?**
A: Forging progress is based on current ownership. If you transfer NFTs out, they won't count toward your forging requirements.

### Leaderboard Questions

**Q: How do I get on the leaderboard?**
A: Connect your wallet and create a username. Guest players don't appear on leaderboards.

**Q: How are leaderboard rankings determined?**
A: By total seasonal points, with tie-breakers: NFTs minted, perfect scores, average answer time, sessions used, and timestamp.

**Q: When does the leaderboard reset?**
A: At the start of each new season (every 3 months). Your all-time stats are preserved.

**Q: What does the season winner get?**
A: The top player on the global leaderboard receives an exclusive, developer-created NFT.

**Q: Can I see past season leaderboards?**
A: Yes! Go to the Leaderboard page and select "Past Seasons" to view historical standings.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Why is my transaction taking so long?**
A: Cardano blockchain confirmations typically take 2-5 minutes. During high network traffic, it may take up to 10 minutes.

**Q: My NFT isn't showing in my wallet. What should I do?**
A: Wait 5-10 minutes for your wallet to sync. Try refreshing your wallet. Check cardanoscan.io to verify the mint was successful.

**Q: I'm getting a "wallet not detected" error. Help!**
A: Ensure your wallet extension is installed and enabled. Try refreshing the page. Make sure you're using a supported wallet (Lace, Eternl, Nami, Typhon).

**Q: Can I use a hardware wallet?**
A: Yes! If your software wallet (like Eternl or Lace) supports hardware wallet integration, you can use it with TriviaNFT.

**Q: Is my wallet information secure?**
A: Yes. TriviaNFT never has access to your private keys or seed phrase. We only read your stake key for identification and request transaction signatures when you approve them.

**Q: What data does TriviaNFT collect?**
A: We collect gameplay data (scores, timing), wallet stake keys (for identification), and optional email addresses. We never collect private keys or seed phrases.

### Account Questions

**Q: Can I change my username?**
A: Not currently. Choose your username carefully when creating your profile.

**Q: Can I delete my account?**
A: Yes, contact support at support@trivianft.com. Note that your NFTs will remain in your wallet.

**Q: I lost access to my wallet. Can I recover my account?**
A: Your account is tied to your wallet's stake key. If you have your seed phrase, you can restore your wallet and regain access. Without it, the account cannot be recovered.

**Q: Can I have multiple accounts?**
A: Each wallet (stake key) can have one account. You can use multiple wallets for multiple accounts, but this may violate our Fair Play policy if used to circumvent daily limits.
